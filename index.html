    About Us

    Get to Know PRU Robotics

    Founded in 2016, PRU Robotics is one of the longest-running and most active student clubs at Piri Reis University. Our mission is to encourage a strong culture of innovation, research and institutional memory in robotics and autonomous systems.

    As part of our commitment to transparency and collaboration, we organize our projects and codebases under the PRU Robotics GitHub Organization, where we proudly embrace open-source development.

    • Participating in national and international robotics competitions.
    • Developing autonomous land, surface, underwater and aerial vehicles.
    • Encouraging teamwork, innovation, and hands-on engineering experience.

    Want to contribute or collaborate with us? Reach out — we’re always open to new challenges and ideas!

    Our teams continues to represent Piri Reis University in high-level competitions such as TEKNOFEST and NJORD Challenge develops real-world robotics solutions driven by student passion and dedication.
